Your Teacher

A musician who loves to teach.

A
Amelia
Violin Teacher · Trinity Music Academy

Amelia began her musical journey at the piano before finding her true passion in the violin at age seven. Recognised for exceptional talent, she was awarded a full scholarship to the Pettman National Junior Academy of Music, and earned her ATCL Diploma with Distinction from Trinity College London at just fourteen years old.

During her studies she served as Concertmaster, competed at the Chamber Music National Semi-finals, and received a Soloist Award at KBB Music. She brings this depth of experience — and genuine warmth — into every lesson she teaches.
